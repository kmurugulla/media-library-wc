Media Library - Sitemap Source

Sitemap Integration

This example demonstrates scanning websites by parsing their sitemap.xml files. The sitemap source automatically discovers all pages and extracts media files from them.

Note: The scanning process has two phases: First, the system discovers and fetches the sitemap (this may take 10-30 seconds due to CORS proxy usage). Then, it scans each page for media files. Form fields will be disabled during sitemap discovery to prevent conflicts.
